Websites are no longer only viewed on desktop computers with large screen monitors. Today's websites are viewed on a wide range of devices with varying screen sizes, ranging from small screened mobile devices like phones to mid-sized screens found on tablets and laptops, to the aforementioned wide screen desktop monitors. To be successful online today, a website must accommodate all of these different screen sizes with a design that reflows its layout for each of them and works well for each of them. This is accomplished through the use of a technique known as responsive web design.
